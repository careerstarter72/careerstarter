For passive job seekers

Finding your next opportunity is a full time job! Tailoring, fixing, and changing resumes to fit every position you apply for is time consuming. If you are doing this on a job board, the average response rate is around 1%-4%.

Enter the world of a truly passive job search where employers reach out to you, based on your preset criteria and proven skills and achievements.

  • No more resumes, ever.
  • No more cover letters, ever.
  • No more looking for jobs on multiple job boards.
  • No more time wasted filling out applications before knowing the Employerâ€™s intent.
  • A completely private portfolio, built by you and only seen by you.
  • No bias from employers, the entire database is blind until you get to the on-site interview.
  • Job candidates on CareerStarter are the real deal, the best talent available. Your skills and achievements are proven by documents you provide which are vetted by us. As an added option, we run a background check on you annually, this tells Employers; you are ready to hit the ground running.

Simply build a private portfolio by uploading the following documents:

  • current and past performance reviews from employers.
  • Proof of education.
  • Proof of volunteering.
  • Additional training.
  • Military service.
  • Pay package information.

You also get to choose under what criteria you will accept an interview, such criteria includes:

  • Salary requested.
  • Work location by zip code or state.
  • Work and life integration.
  • Job positions/titles.
  • Vacation.
  • Sick days.

We then combine the data with powerful algorithms and analytics to recommend you blindly to employers. Next, interested employers send a phone interview request through our platform; the request will give you the option to choose a time and date within a one-week period.Once, you accept the phone interview request, we release a blind transcript of the verified documents you provided with a system generated ID number.

All this done effortlessly, so you can spend more time enjoying life.
