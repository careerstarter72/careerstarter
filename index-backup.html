Our Features

Customizable Dashboard

Comparison with peers through a customizable dashboard helping visualize career progression/salary/education etc.

Career Membership

Career coach provided with paid membership, to help increase ranking in employer queries. Free for early adopters.

Career Training

Recommended training specific to your career path based on employer and input of peers, along with where to get it. To help keep you competitive.

Interview Sessions

Easily view and accept interview request from employers through app based on available times provided by employer, after

Google Maps

Google Maps, to see how your commute will be if you get hired.

Employers Contact

Specific criteria for which employers can initiate contact. Such criteria include; salary, work flexibility, location etc. Benefits and terms are agreed upon before employers contact you.

Employee Surveys

Current and former employee satisfaction surveys, with employment verified by W2s or performance reviews. To get you a real and accurate picture of what it is like to work for the potential employer.

Complete privacy

Portfolio can only be viewed by the job seeker. A sterilized transcript of the verified documents in the Portfolio is released to the potential employer only when the job seeker accepts an interview.

View company’s social media YouTube, help, WalkScore.
