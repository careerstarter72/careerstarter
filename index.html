For employers

CareerStarter does not rely on resumes or any form of self-reporting. Instead, we track each candidate's performance, based on verifiable documents they provide...

CareerStarter does not rely on resumes or any form of self-reporting. Instead, we track each candidate's performance, based on verifiable documents they provide. This information is combined with our proprietary algorithm and industry analytics to recommend job candidates based on real data - candidates that fit your needs.

Even the most experienced human resources managers are prone to biases. The pressure to fill a position quickly demands a fast, and often instinctive decision. And after making that decision, 86% of employers have uncovered lies or misrepresentations on the candidates' resumes or job applications. CareerStarter mitigates this risk through a blind application process of pre-qualified candidates.

Query the job candidate database with filters like, expected salary range, years of experience or specific abilities. Select the most qualified candidates, provide a schedule for phone interviews, and start meeting the candidates. Once the candidate chooses a day and time, we release a blind transcript of verified documents provided by the candidate with a system generated ID number.

After the phone interviews, further refine your list of candidates, and use our easy to use scheduling system for on-site interviews.

For passive job seekers

Finding your next opportunity is a full time job! Tailoring, editing, and changing resumes to fit every position you apply for is time consuming...

Finding your next opportunity is a full time job! Tailoring, editing, and changing resumes to fit every position you apply for is time consuming. If you are doing this on a job board, the average response rate is around 1%-4%.
Enter the world of a truly passive job search where employers reach out to you, based on your preset criteria, proven skills, and achievements.
• No more resumes, ever.
• No more cover letters, ever.
• No more looking for jobs on multiple job boards.
• No more time wasted filling out applications before knowing the employer’s intent.
• No bias from employers, the entire database is blind until you get to the on-site interview.
• A private portfolio, built by you and only seen by you.
• Your skills and achievements are proven by documents you provide. As an added option, we can run a background check annually, this tells employers that you are prepared for the next opportunity.

Our Features

Career Coaching

As a paid member benefit, you will receive personalized advice to help you stand out and increase your ranking in employee queries.
